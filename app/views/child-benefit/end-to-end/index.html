View the digital version of the claim form

Why is this needed?

We know some people might have trouble reading a PDF because the text doesn't adjust to fit the screen when the PDF file is enlarged. This means that users may have to scroll horizontally, making it more difficult to read.

To make it easier, we offer digital versions of claim form info and other details that are usually shown in a PDF. This includes official use info for claims about children born outside the UK when returning documents.
