Application for Child Benefit Received

What happens next

We've sent your application to Child Benefit Service.

HMRC will send you a letter to tell you:

  • if you will get Child Benefit
  • how much you will be paid (opens in new tab)
  • when you will get your first payment (opens in new tab)

You may get your first payment before you get your letter.

What you need to do

Your partner must set up and fill in a Self-Assessment tax return (opens in new tab) to pay the tax charge.

Use the Child Benefit tax calculator (opens in new tab) to find out how much tax your partner may have to pay.

HMRC may call you if they need more information.

If your circumstances change

You must report any changes to your family life or child's life (opens in new tab) to the Child Benefit Office, as changes in circumstances may affect your Child Benefit tax charge (opens in new tab).
