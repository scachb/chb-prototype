What to do now

After you have printed this form and signed and dated the Declaration, return the form with any documents we need to:

Child Benefit Office (GB)
Washington
NEWCASTLE UPON TYNE
NE88 1ZD

An extra sheet with your name and address will be printed with your claim form, if you are sending us any documents. Make sure that this is sent to us with your claim. We use it to send any documents back to you.

You’ll need a stamp on the outside of your envelope to post your form and documents to us, don’t send the stamp to us separately. Make sure you pay the correct postage.

What happens next

We’ll review your claim and write to tell you if you can get Child Benefit and how much you’ll get. We’ll normally return any documents you have sent us within 4 weeks.

If you need further assistance with your claim, contact the Child Benefit helpline.
