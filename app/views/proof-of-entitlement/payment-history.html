Find out when you'll be paid next

If you're paid every 4 weeks, you can find out when you'll be paid next by counting 4 weeks forward from your last payment.

You'll be paid earlier if the payment's due on a bank holiday (opens in a new tab).

Child Benefit is paid weekly or every 4 weeks. It is not paid by calendar month.
